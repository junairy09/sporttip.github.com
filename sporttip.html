🏀 Basketball
Master the Crossover Dribble
⭐ Beginner — 15 min drill

Practice the crossover at slow speed first — control beats speed every time. Keep your eyes up, dribble below the knee, and shift your weight with each switch.

🏀 Basketball
3-Point Shooting Form Fix
⭐⭐ Intermediate — 20 min drill

Align your shooting elbow under the ball, not to the side. Film yourself from the front and side — most shooters don't realize their elbow flares until they see the footage.

👤 Coach Lisa T. Read More →
🏀 Basketball
Explosive First Step
⭐⭐⭐ Advanced — 30 min drill

Use a "jab-and-go" sequence with cone work. Start in triple threat, jab hard, then explode past. Drive off the outside foot for maximum lateral force — not the inside.

👤 Coach Dev P. Read More →
⚽ Football
Perfect the Weak Foot Pass
⭐ Beginner — 20 min drill

Plant your strong foot beside the ball, lock the ankle of your weak foot and strike through the center. Dedicate 10 minutes each session purely to your weaker side — it compounds fast.

👤 Coach Ana S. Read More →
⚽ Football
Pressing Trap Coordination
⭐⭐ Intermediate — Team drill

The trigger for your press must be agreed before the game. Common triggers: goalkeeper receives, center-back turns away. Practice the 3-second counter-press window after every turnover.

👤 Coach Fernan G. Read More →
⚽ Football
Long Ball Aerial Control
⭐⭐ Intermediate — 25 min drill

Get side-on early, watch the flight, cushion with the thigh or chest — never let the ball bounce to an opponent. Practice with a lofted machine or a tall partner serving from distance.

👤 Coach Marta V. Read More →
🎾 Tennis
Topspin Forehand Mechanics
⭐⭐ Intermediate — 30 min drill

Brush up the back of the ball, not through it. Drop the racket head below ball height early, then accelerate steeply upward. A low-to-high swing path generates the spin — not wrist flicking.

👤 Coach Priya K. Read More →
🎾 Tennis
Second Serve Confidence
⭐⭐⭐ Advanced — 20 min drill

Commit to kick spin by tossing slightly behind your head and snapping the racket from 7 o'clock to 1 o'clock. Practice 50 second serves in a row before you mix in first serves again.

👤 Coach James H. Read More →
🎾 Tennis
Net Approach Off Short Ball
⭐⭐ Intermediate — 15 min drill

When the short ball lands inside the service box, approach deep to the corner and split-step as you close the net. Punch the volley crosscourt — don't go down the line from a wide position.

👤 Coach Elena V. Read More →
🏊 Swimming
Fix Your Freestyle Rotation
⭐ Beginner — 20 min drill

Rotate from the hips, not the shoulders. Use a kick board drill: one arm stretched forward, rotate to breathe, then switch. Aim for 45° rotation — not flat, not rolling too far.

👤 Coach Sam W. Read More →
🏊 Swimming
Underwater Dolphin Kick
⭐⭐⭐ Advanced — 25 min drill

Power comes from the core undulation, not the knees. Keep legs close together, initiate from the chest, and let the wave travel down. Practice streamline dolphin kick sets off every wall during warm-up.

👤 Coach Nina O. Read More →
🏊 Swimming
Flip Turn Efficiency
⭐⭐ Intermediate — 15 min drill

Hit the wall at your peak velocity — don't glide in. Count your strokes from the "T" mark each session until it becomes automatic. Push off with both feet simultaneously and hold streamline for 1–2 seconds.

👤 Coach Rick B. Read More →
🏐 Volleyball
Platform Passing Basics
⭐ Beginner — 20 min drill

Clasp your hands firmly, keep elbows straight, and contact the ball on your forearms — not your wrists. Get your platform to the ball early. Most passing errors happen because players move their arms instead of their feet.

👤 Coach Caro L. Read More →
🏐 Volleyball
Attack Approach Timing
⭐⭐ Intermediate — 25 min drill

A 4-step approach gives the most power: left-right-left-right for right-handers. Time your last two steps to plant just as the setter's hands release. Early approaches kill your jump — patience is key.

👤 Coach Bruno A. Read More →
🏐 Volleyball
Jump Serve Power & Control
⭐⭐⭐ Advanced — 30 min drill

Toss high and in front of your hitting shoulder — not above your head. Approach like an attack, contact at your peak with a full arm swing. Aim for topspin; it drops fast and is harder to pass.

👤 Coach Yuki N. Read More →
🏃 Running
Improve Running Economy
⭐ Beginner — Daily habit

Run tall, relax your shoulders, and land with your foot under your hip — not in front of you. A slight forward lean from the ankles (not the waist) naturally drives momentum without extra effort.

👤 Coach Jade M. Read More →
🏃 Running
Interval Training Protocol
⭐⭐ Intermediate — 45 min session

Try the classic 8×400m at 5K pace with 90-second rest. The key is keeping the last rep as fast as the first. If you fade badly, you started too hot — back off 3–5 seconds per 400 next time.

👤 Coach Tom F. Read More →
🏃 Running
Marathon Pacing Strategy
⭐⭐⭐ Advanced — Race strategy

Run the first 20 miles with your head, the last 10K with your heart. Target even splits or a very slight negative split. Going out 5 seconds per mile too fast in the first half can cost you 2–3 minutes in the back half.

👤 Coach Keiko R. Read More →

The Mikan Drill:
Dominate the Paint

Named after Hall of Famer George Mikan, this relentless close-range finishing drill builds touch, timing, and two-handed coordination. Run it for 60 seconds non-stop and your layup percentage will jump within two weeks.

  • Start under the basket on the right side, shoot a right-handed layup
  • Without letting the ball bounce, catch it and shoot a left-handed layup on the other side
  • Alternate continuously for 60 seconds at full pace
  • Rest 30 seconds and repeat 4 rounds
  • Advance: add a reverse layup variation on every third attempt
